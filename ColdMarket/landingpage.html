About Us

ColdMarket is revolutionizing the cold market industry with our specialized e-commerce platform.

Founded in 2020, we recognized the unique challenges faced by businesses in the cold market sector. Traditional e-commerce solutions weren't meeting the specific needs of temperature-sensitive product transactions.

Our team of industry experts and technology innovators came together to create a platform that addresses these challenges head-on. We combine cutting-edge technology with deep industry knowledge to provide a seamless experience for both buyers and sellers.

Today, ColdMarket connects thousands of businesses across the globe, facilitating millions in transactions while maintaining the highest standards of quality and security.
